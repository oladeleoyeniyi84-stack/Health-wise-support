Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app ask for Motion, Fitness, or Location?

These permissions enable step counting, workout routes, and context-aware reminders. You can change them anytime in Settings.

How do I back up or sync my data?

If you’re signed in, your core data can sync securely. Check the in-app Settings > Backup.

I’m not receiving reminders.

  • Ensure notifications are enabled in system Settings.
  • Disable Focus/Do Not Disturb during reminder times.
  • Keep Background App Refresh on.

How do refunds work?

Purchases are handled by Apple/Google. Request refunds through your store account order history.
