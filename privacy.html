Health-wise respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how it’s used.

    • Motion/Fitness — to measure steps/activities.
    • Notifications — to send reminders.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (tracking, insights, reminders).
  • Improve app performance and reliability.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is not shared with third parties except as required by law.

Security

We use platform security best practices. You can delete the app to remove on-device data.
