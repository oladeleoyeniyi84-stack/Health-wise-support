Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need motion & location access?

These permissions are required to provide accurate health and fitness tracking.

Why am I not getting reminders?

Ensure notifications are enabled in both system settings and the app’s preferences.

How do I back up or sync my data?

Your data automatically syncs to your Health-wise account when connected to Wi-Fi.
