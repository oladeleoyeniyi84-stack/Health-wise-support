Frequently Asked Questions

What devices and iOS versions are supported?

Health-wise supports iPhone running iOS 16 or later. Performance may vary by device model.

How do I start tracking steps, runs, or routes?

Open the app → grant Motion & Fitness and Location permissions when prompted. You can adjust these later in Settings > Privacy & Security.

Does the app provide medical advice?

No. Health-wise offers wellness information only and is not a medical device. For health concerns, consult a licensed professional.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone to view, change, or cancel.

What data do you collect and how is it used?

We collect only what’s needed to deliver features (e.g., activity metrics, optional location for routes). See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Why isn’t GPS tracking working?

Ensure Location is set to “While Using the App” (and “Precise Location” ON), Low Power Mode is OFF, and you have a clear view of the sky during outdoor activities.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Reach us via YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL or open an issue on our support site repository.
