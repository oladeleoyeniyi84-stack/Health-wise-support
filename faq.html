Frequently Asked Questions

How do I grant Motion & Fitness and Location permissions?

Open the Health-wise app > follow the setup prompts. You can also enable later from your device Settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Motion & Fitness / Location Services.
  • Android: Settings → Apps → Health-wise → Permissions.

Why am I not receiving reminders?

  • Confirm Notifications are enabled for Health-wise.
  • Disable Focus/Do Not Disturb during reminder times.
  • Keep Low Power Mode off for reliable scheduling.

How do I back up my data?

Health-wise syncs your key preferences securely. For exports or account-level help, contact us via the contact form.

Still stuck?

Send us details (device, OS, steps to reproduce) and we'll help quickly.
