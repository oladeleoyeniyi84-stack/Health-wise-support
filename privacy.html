Privacy Policy

Effective: Aug 16, 2025

Summary: We collect only what’s needed to run the app and improve your experience. You control your data.

Information We Collect

  • Account & profile (if created): name, email.
  • Health & activity data you choose to record (steps, workouts, mood, hydration).
  • Device data: app version, OS, crash logs.
  • Location (optional) for GPS routes and nearby features.

How We Use Data

  • Provide core features (tracking, insights, reminders).
  • Sync across devices and enable backup/restore.
  • Diagnostics, crash reporting, and to prevent abuse.

Your Choices

  • Manage permissions anytime in system Settings.
  • Export or delete your data by contacting us at the address on Contact.
  • Opt out of analytics where offered in-app.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above. We use industry-standard safeguards and never sell personal data.

Children

Health-wise is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to remove it.
