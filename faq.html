FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Why do you need Motion & Fitness and Location?

We use these permissions to track activity and routes for wellness features. You can manage them in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security.

  • Motion & Fitness: Daily activity & steps.
  • Location: Accurate GPS for walking/jogging routes.

I’m not getting reminders

  1. Open iOS Settings > Notifications > Health-wise and allow alerts.
  2. Disable Focus/Do Not Disturb during reminder times.
  3. Ensure Low Power Mode is off for background delivery.

Data sync & backups

Health-wise uses on-device storage and iCloud (when available) for backups. Sign in with the same Apple ID to restore.
