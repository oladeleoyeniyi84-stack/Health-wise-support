Quick Troubleshooting

  • Make sure you’re on the latest app version from the App Store.
  • Check permissions (Motion & Fitness, Location, Notifications) in iOS Settings.
  • For GPS: disable Low Power Mode; ensure good outdoor signal.
  • Restart the app or your device if the issue persists.
