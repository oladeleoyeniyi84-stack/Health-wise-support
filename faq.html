Frequently Asked Questions

Need more help? Email support@example.com
Does Health-wise provide medical advice?

No. Health-wise offers general wellness features and insights. It is not a medical device and should not replace professional medical advice.

What permissions does the app request and why?

Depending on the features you use, the app may request access to Motion & Fitness, Location (for GPS tracking), Notifications (for reminders), and Health data (where available) to provide accurate activity and wellness tracking.

How do I improve GPS accuracy for walks or runs?

Enable Location Services, keep Low Power Mode off during workouts, and ensure you have a clear view of the sky for best GPS signal.

How do I export or delete my data?

Open the app > Settings > Data & Privacy to request an export or deletion. You can also contact us at the support email.

Will you add dark mode?

Yes — Health-wise supports both light and dark appearance and will follow your system preference.

How can I report a bug or request a feature?

Send a brief description, screenshots, and device info to support@example.com.
