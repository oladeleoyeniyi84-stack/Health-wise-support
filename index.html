Quick help

Permissions to allow

  • Motion & Fitness for step counting and activity.
  • Location (While Using) for GPS walks/runs.
  • Notifications for reminders.
  • Health (where available) for metrics sync.
Learn more

Improve GPS accuracy

  • Disable Low Power Mode during workouts.
  • Keep the screen off but phone unlocked in a pocket/armband.
  • Start in an open area for a strong initial lock.

Common fixes

  • Force-quit and reopen the app.
  • Reboot the device after granting permissions.
  • Update to the latest app & iOS versions.
