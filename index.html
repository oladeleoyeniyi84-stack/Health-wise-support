About Health-wise

Health-wise simplifies wellness by combining powerful tracking tools with AI-driven insights. Designed for both men and women, it helps you build sustainable habits, stay motivated, and take control of your health journey.

  • Track activity, nutrition, hydration
  • Monitor stress, mood, and sleep patterns
  • Personalized AI-driven health insights
  • GPS for walking, jogging, workout tracking
  • Smart reminders & notifications
  • Light and dark mode

FAQ

  • Does Health-wise require an account? The initial version stores data on device. Future versions may add optional cloud sync.
  • What permissions are needed? Motion/fitness, location (for GPS tracking), and notifications (for reminders). All are optional and can be changed in Settings.
  • Which devices are supported? iPhone running iOS 15+.
